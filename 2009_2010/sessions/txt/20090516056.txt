   The Senate resolved itself into a Committee of the Whole, with Senator Murphy in the chair.
   After some time spent therein, the committee arose, and Senator Murphy reported that the
committee had considered the following:
   H.F. No. 1276, which the committee recommends to pass.
   H.F. No. 705, which the committee recommends to pass, subject to the following motion:

                                     RECONSIDERATION

   Having voted on the prevailing side, Senator Lynch moved that the vote whereby the Prettner
Solon amendment to H.F. No. 705 was adopted by the Senate on May 15, 2009, be now reconsidered.
   The question was taken on the adoption of the motion.

   Senator Betzold moved that the Senate do now recess subject to the call of the President. The
motion prevailed.
