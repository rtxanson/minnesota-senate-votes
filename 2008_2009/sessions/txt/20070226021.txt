   Page 3, delete section 5 and insert:
  "Sec. 5. [124D.1625] EXPANDING MINNESOTA  DEPARTMENT   OF
EDUCATION DEVELOPMENTAL ASSESSMENT ADMINISTERED TO ENTERING
KINDERGARTNERS.
    Subdivision 1. Establishment. The commissioner of education shall implement a kindergarten
readiness assessment, based on the 2002-2003 model the department developed for school readiness
studies, to expand to all school districts the developmental assessment that districts voluntarily

   (1) in 2009, assess up to 30 percent of children;

   (3) in 2011, assess up to 75 percent of children; and
   (4) in 2012, assess up to 100 percent of children.

legislature by January 1 of the next year.
   EFFECTIVE DATE. This section is effective July 1, 2007."

  And when so amended the bill do pass and be re-referred to the Committee on Finance.
