    Senator Vandeveer from the Committee on Local Government and Elections, to which was

    S.F. No. 124: A bill for an act relating to local government; providing for terms for members

subdivision.

clerical officer comply with Minnesota Statutes, section 645.021, subdivisions 2 and 3, and applies
retroactively to all terms beginning on or after January 1, 2011."
   And when so amended the bill do pass. Amendments adopted. Report adopted.
