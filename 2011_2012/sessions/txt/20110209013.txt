                              MOTIONS AND RESOLUTIONS

   Senator Reinert moved that the name of Senator Bonoff be added as a co-author to S.F. No. 220.
The motion prevailed.

motion prevailed.
   Senator Vandeveer moved that the name of Senator Sheran be added as a co-author to S.F. No.
254. The motion prevailed.
   Senator Wiger moved that the names of Senators Olson and Rest be added as co-authors to S.F.

S.F. No. 258. The motion prevailed.

prevailed.

2011. The motion prevailed.
